F.A.Q

»How long does it take to design a website?

We keep your schedule in mind when we take on your project. At the start of each project, we create a timeline. The most common delays typically come in the final stages of the project while waiting for content or images from the client.


»How much do you charge for your services?

The costs associated with our services vary accoring to the scope of the project. A simple website with a few static pages typically costs around $300, whereas a website with several pages and custom images could cost anywhere between $500 and $3000. As the complexity of the site increases, so does the cost. Our print design services follow a similiar scale. Don't hesitate to contact us for a free quote. Currently, we're offering discounted pricing in exchange for the privledge of featuring your site in our Design Portfolio.


»What if I do not like the Design?

Before we create the website or print materials, we send you mock-up images. If you do not like the suggested design, we will consult with you as to what changes need to be made, then design a second mock-up for your consideration. Our goal is to create a website tailored to your specific needs.

 

»Who owns the website once the website design is completed?

The website belongs to you - we send you a copy of the website on cd. This copy is for your retention and should you wish to use another web design or web hosting company - you are completely at liberty to take this copy to them for them to use.


»Can you provide all our web design and hosting services?

Yes - we are your one stop company to provide all your web services. No more - not knowing who to call if you have problems - we can register your domain name - design your website - provide your web hosting and arrange your email accounts for you.
