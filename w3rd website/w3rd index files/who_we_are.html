Who We Are

W3rd Designs, Inc. is a freelance web design company located in the Dallas/Fort Worth area. Owned and operated by husband and wife team, Jeremy and Jessica Kontelis, we offer customized design services for small businesses and individuals, including website development, print design, and other digital media projects upon request.

 

We also offer digital teaching portfolio development and support. Jessica, our content writer and designer, is a current Composition & Rhetoric Ph.D student, and has a vested interest in digital teaching portfolio design.

 

We're not your average design company. All our work is done in-house by Jeremy and Jessica, which means our work is 100% consistent from start to finish and your needs are treated with the respect they deserve. Because we're a relatively new company, we're currently offering discounted pricing in exchange for the priveledge of featuring your site and/or print materials on our website.
